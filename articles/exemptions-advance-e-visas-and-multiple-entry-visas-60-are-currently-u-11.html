Introduction to Egypt Visa Policy

Egypt has long been a popular destination for tourists, attracting millions of visitors each year with its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture. To facilitate travel, the Egyptian government offers a visa on arrival option for citizens of eligible countries. This allows travelers to obtain their visa upon arrival at the airport, making the process more convenient and streamlined. However, with the recent update, it's crucial to understand the new visa policy and how it will impact your travel plans.

Understanding the Visa on Arrival Fee Increase

The Egypt visa on arrival fee increase, which came into effect on March 1, 2026, introduces a new fee of $30 for tourists. This represents a $5 increase from the previous fee of $25. It's essential to note that this surcharge applies to visa on arrival applications and does not affect advance e-Visas and multiple-entry visas, which remain unchanged at $60. As you prepare for your trip to Egypt, it's vital to consider the payment options for your visa on arrival. Exemptions and Special Cases

While the visa on arrival fee increase applies to most tourists, there are exemptions and special cases to be aware of. Exemptions: Advance e-Visas and multiple-entry visas ($60) are currently unaffected by this specific surcharge. If you're planning to visit Egypt for business or other purposes, it's essential to check the relevant visa requirements and fees. Additionally, citizens of certain countries may be eligible for visa-free travel or have different visa requirements, so it's crucial to verify the specific regulations for your nationality.

Important tip: Always check the official government website or consult with the Egyptian embassy for the most up-to-date information on visa requirements and fees to avoid any unexpected surprises during your trip.

Preparing for Your Trip to Egypt

With the new visa policy in place, it's more important than ever to be prepared for your trip to Egypt. We recommend checking the official government website for the latest information on visa requirements, fees, and any travel restrictions. Additionally, consider the following tips to ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey:

  • Verify your eligibility for a visa on arrival or advance e-Visa
  • Check the required documents, such as a valid passport and proof of onward travel
  • Ensure you have sufficient funds for the visa fee and any additional expenses
  • Research any specific requirements for your nationality or purpose of travel
