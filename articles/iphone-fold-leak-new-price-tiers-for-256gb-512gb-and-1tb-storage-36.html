Introduction to the iPhone Fold Leak

The iPhone Fold leak, which originated from a post on Instant Digital Weibo, suggests that Apple's upcoming foldable iPhone will be available in three storage options: 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB. According to the leak, the pricing for these storage options will be tiered, with the base model starting at a lower price point and increasing as the storage capacity increases. As we continue to analyze the leak, it's essential to note that these prices are subject to change and may not reflect the final pricing for the iPhone Fold.

iPhone Fold Storage Options and Pricing

Based on the leak, the iPhone Fold storage options and pricing are as follows:

Storage Option Price (Yuan) Price (USD)
256GB 8,999 yuan approximately $1,340 USD
512GB 10,499 yuan approximately $1,560 USD
1TB 12,999 yuan approximately $1,930 USD
