The New 2027 Honda Civic Is an Insight at Heart

By Marcus J. Holloway Senior Tech Educator
Published: 16 Apr, 2026 Updated: 16 Apr, 2026 7 Min Read

With over 24 million units sold worldwide, the Honda Civic is one of the best-selling cars of all time. However, the upcoming 2027 Honda Civic is set to challenge common assumptions about the model, as it takes a significant step towards becoming an Insight at heart. By incorporating the Honda Insight's hybrid technology and design elements, the new Civic promises to deliver up to 50% better fuel economy and a more refined driving experience.

The 2027 Honda Civic is expected to feature a 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle engine, paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 151 horsepower and 187 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain is designed to provide a seamless and efficient driving experience, making it an attractive option for those seeking a compact car with a conscience. As the automotive industry continues to evolve, the 2027 Honda Civic is poised to play a significant role in shaping the future of compact cars.

Design and Features

The 2027 Honda Civic boasts a sleek and modern design, with a 0.25 drag coefficient that reduces wind resistance and improves fuel efficiency. The car's exterior features a bold front grille, LED headlights, and a trunk lid spoiler that enhances its aerodynamic profile. Inside, the 2027 Honda Civic offers a spacious and well-appointed cabin, with premium materials, heated seats, and a 7-inch touchscreen display that supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

In terms of safety features, the 2027 Honda Civic comes equipped with a 5-star overall safety rating, thanks to its advanced airbag system, electronic stability control, and rearview camera. Additionally, the car features a range of driver-assistance technologies, including forward collision warning, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control.

Interior and Comfort

The 2027 Honda Civic offers a comfortable and feature-rich interior, with ample legroom and cargo space. The car's seats are designed to provide excellent support and comfort, while the dual-zone automatic climate control system ensures a pleasant driving experience in any weather condition. With a 15.1 cubic foot trunk and a 60/40 split-folding rear seat, the 2027 Honda Civic is ideal for road trips and daily commutes alike.

Performance and Efficiency

The 2027 Honda Civic is powered by a hybrid powertrain that combines a 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle engine with an electric motor. This setup produces a combined 151 horsepower and 187 lb-ft of torque, making it an excellent option for those seeking a balance between performance and efficiency. With an estimated 44 mpg in the city and 47 mpg on the highway, the 2027 Honda Civic is an attractive choice for commuters and environmentally conscious drivers.
