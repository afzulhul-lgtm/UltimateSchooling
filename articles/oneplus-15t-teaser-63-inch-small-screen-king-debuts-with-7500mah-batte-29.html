OnePlus 15T Teaser: 6.3-Inch 'Small Screen King' Debuts With 7,500mAh Battery and 1.1mm Bezels

By Marcus J. Holloway Senior Tech Educator
Published: May 04, 2026 Updated: May 04, 2026 5 Min Read

As the tech world waits with bated breath for the latest flagship releases, a new contender has emerged to shake things up. In a surprise move, OnePlus has unveiled a teaser for its upcoming 15T model, touting a compact 6.3-inch screen and an impressive 7,500mAh battery.

The timing of this release is particularly noteworthy, as the market for smaller, yet powerful smartphones is experiencing a resurgence in popularity. With many consumers seeking a more streamlined device that still packs a punch, the OnePlus 15T is poised to capitalize on this trend.

What Happened — The Full Story

The OnePlus 15T teaser reveals a device with a striking 6.3-inch screen, earning it the moniker "Small Screen King." This compact design is paired with an impressive 7,500mAh battery, promising extended usage times without the need for frequent recharging. Additionally, the device boasts slim 1.1mm bezels, further enhancing its aesthetic appeal.

OnePlus has carefully crafted the 15T to address the needs of users seeking a balance between portability and performance. By incorporating a large battery into a relatively small form factor, the company aims to provide a unique selling proposition in a crowded market.

The teaser itself has generated significant buzz, with many fans and tech enthusiasts taking to social media to express their excitement and speculate about the device's potential features and capabilities.

The Background You Need to Know

To understand the significance of the OnePlus 15T, it's essential to consider the current state of the smartphone market. In recent years, there has been a shift towards larger, more feature-rich devices, often at the expense of portability and battery life. However, a growing segment of consumers is now seeking a more balanced approach, prioritizing compact designs without sacrificing performance.

This trend is driven, in part, by the increasing demand for devices that can keep up with the rigors of daily life without requiring frequent recharging. The OnePlus 15T, with its large battery and efficient design, is well-positioned to meet this need.

Furthermore, the device's 1.1mm bezels demonstrate OnePlus' commitment to creating a visually stunning product that also provides a premium user experience.

A closer examination of the OnePlus 15T's specifications reveals a device that is both powerful and efficient. The 7,500mAh battery, in particular, is a notable feature, promising extended usage times and reduced downtime.
