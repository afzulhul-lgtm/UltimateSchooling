Introduction to the Huawei Pura 90 Chip

The Huawei Pura 90 chip is built on the SMIC N+2 process node, which provides a significant boost to performance and power efficiency. This, combined with the Kirin 9010S's 8-core, 12-thread design, makes for a potent combination that's capable of handling even the most demanding tasks. But what about the GPU? The Pura 90 features the MaleoON 920C, which offers a significant improvement over its predecessor in terms of graphics processing power.

One of the most interesting aspects of the Pura 90 is its ability to work seamlessly with HarmonyOS 6.1, Huawei's latest operating system. This integration allows for cloud collaboration and hardware-software optimization, making for a more efficient and responsive user experience. But how does this impact real-world performance, and what can we expect from devices powered by the Pura 90?

Huawei Pura 90 vs Pura 80: What's Changed?

So, what exactly sets the Pura 90 apart from its predecessor, the Pura 80? For starters, the Pura 90 offers a 15% performance boost, thanks to the improved Kirin 9010S chip.
