Introduction to iPhone Fold Display Specs

The iPhone Fold's 7.8-inch inner screen is expected to be one of the largest and most impressive displays on a foldable device to date. With a rumored aspect ratio of 22:9, this screen will provide an immersive experience for users, perfect for watching videos, browsing the web, and gaming. The 5.5-inch cover display, on the other hand, will offer a more traditional smartphone experience, allowing users to quickly check notifications, make calls, and send texts without having to open the device.
