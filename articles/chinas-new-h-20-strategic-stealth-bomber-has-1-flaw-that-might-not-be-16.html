The H-20 is also equipped with advanced avionics, including a phased array radar system and a advanced electronic warfare system. Design and Development

The H-20 is a indigenous Chinese design that was first unveiled in 2019. The aircraft is powered by four WS-18 engines and has a length of 108 feet. The H-20's wingspan is 131 feet and it has a maximum takeoff weight of 230,000 pounds. The aircraft is equipped with a advanced fly-by-wire system and a glass cockpit.
