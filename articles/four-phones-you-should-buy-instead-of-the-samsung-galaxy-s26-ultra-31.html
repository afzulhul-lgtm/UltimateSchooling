Four phones you should buy instead of the Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra

Ultimate Schooling Editor
13 Mar, 2026 Updated 4 min read

As we dive into the world of flagship smartphones in 2026, the Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is undoubtedly one of the most anticipated devices. However, we believe that there are other phones that can offer better value, camera capabilities, and overall user experience. In this article, we will explore four phones that you should consider instead of the Galaxy S26 Ultra. Our team has carefully evaluated these alternatives, and we are excited to share our findings with you.

Introduction to Galaxy S26 Ultra Alternatives

The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is a powerhouse of a phone, with a large 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED display, up to 16GB of RAM, and a massive 5000mAh battery. However, with a price tag to match its premium features, it may not be the best option for everyone. We have identified four phones that can offer similar or even better features at a lower price point or with more innovative technologies. These phones are the Oppo Find X9 Pro, Vivo X300 Pro, Xiaomi 17 Ultra, and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL.

Top Flagship Phones to Consider in 2026

Our research has shown that these four phones are strong contenders in the flagship market, with unique features that set them apart from the Galaxy S26 Ultra. The Oppo Find X9 Pro, for example, boasts a 50MP primary camera with a 1-inch sensor, while the Vivo X300 Pro has a 48MP camera with a 3D modeling feature. The Xiaomi 17 Ultra and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL also have impressive camera capabilities, with a 50MP primary camera and a 48MP primary camera, respectively.

Camera Comparison: S26 Ultra vs Oppo Find X9 Pro and Vivo X300 Pro

When it comes to camera capabilities, the Oppo Find X9 Pro and Vivo X300 Pro are two of the strongest competitors to the Galaxy S26 Ultra. The Oppo Find X9 Pro has a 50MP primary camera with a 1-inch sensor, which provides exceptional low-light performance and detailed images. The Vivo X300 Pro, on the other hand, has a 48MP camera with a 3D modeling feature, which allows for stunning portrait shots with precise bokeh effect.

When choosing a phone with a great camera, it's essential to consider the sensor size, aperture, and software features. A larger sensor size and wider aperture can significantly improve low-light performance and overall image quality.

Xiaomi 17 Ultra and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL: The Dark Horses

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL are two phones that may not have received as much attention as the other flagships, but they are definitely worth considering. The Xiaomi 17 Ultra has a 50MP primary camera with a 1-inch sensor, similar to the Oppo Find X9 Pro, and is priced significantly lower. The Google Pixel 10 Pro XL, on the other hand, has a 48MP primary camera and is known for its exceptional software features, including AI-powered camera modes and timely updates.

Conclusion

In conclusion, while the Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is an excellent phone, there are other options available that can offer better value, camera capabilities, and overall user experience. The Oppo Find X9 Pro, Vivo X300 Pro, Xiaomi 17 Ultra, and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL are all strong contenders in the flagship market, and we believe that they are worth considering instead of the Galaxy S26 Ultra. We hope that this article has provided you with a comprehensive overview of these alternatives and has helped you make an informed decision when choosing your next smartphone.
