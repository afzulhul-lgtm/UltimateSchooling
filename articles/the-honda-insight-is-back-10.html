What Happened — The Full Story

The Honda Insight was first introduced in 1999 as a two-door hybrid car, but it was discontinued in 2006 due to low sales. However, with the increasing demand for environmentally friendly vehicles, Honda decided to revive the Insight in 2010 as a five-door hybrid car. The new model was well-received, but it was eventually phased out in 2014. The latest iteration of the Honda Insight is expected to build on the success of its predecessors, with improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.
