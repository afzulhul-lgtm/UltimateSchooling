OnePlus 15T Teaser: 6.3-Inch 'Small Screen King' Debuts With 7,500mAh Battery and 1.1mm Bezels

The wait is finally over for tech enthusiasts, as the OnePlus 15T official teaser image has been unveiled, showcasing a stunning 6.3-inch display, a massive 7,500mAh glacier battery, and incredibly narrow 1.1mm bezels. Introduction to the OnePlus 15T

The OnePlus 15T is set to launch in China this March 2026, and we can't wait to get our hands on this device. With its unique green and brown color options, the OnePlus 15T is sure to turn heads. The device has been dubbed the "Small Screen King" due to its compact 6.3-inch display, which is perfect for those who prefer a more manageable phone size without compromising on features. We are excited to see how the OnePlus 15T will compete with other flagships in the market, including the OnePlus 15.

Design and Display Features

The OnePlus 15T boasts an impressive 6.3-inch display, making it the perfect choice for those who want a compact phone without sacrificing screen real estate. The device features incredibly narrow 1.1mm bezels, giving it a sleek and premium look. The phone's design is further enhanced by its unique green and brown color options, which are sure to appeal to those who want a device that stands out from the crowd. As we at Ultimate Schooling always say, "a good design is not just about looks, but also about functionality," and the OnePlus 15T seems to deliver on both fronts.

Camera and Battery Life

The OnePlus 15T features a dual camera setup, including a 3.5x telephoto lens, which is perfect for capturing stunning images and videos. The device is also equipped with a massive 7,500mAh glacier battery, which supports 100W wired and 50W wireless charging. This means that you can enjoy a full day of use on a single charge, and quickly top up your battery when you're on the go. We at Ultimate Schooling are impressed by the OnePlus 15T's battery life, and we're sure that it will be a major selling point for this device.

Performance and Global Availability

The OnePlus 15T is set to launch in China this March 2026, and we expect it to be available globally soon after.
